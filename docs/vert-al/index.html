Box Sizing: Width

  • complete (100% Width)

  • w95 (95% Width)

  • w65 (65% Width)

  • w75 (75% Width)

  • mid (50% Width)

  • wAuto (Automatic Width)

  • w500 (500px Width)

  • w600 (600px Width)

  • w700 (700px Width)

  • w800 (800px Width)
